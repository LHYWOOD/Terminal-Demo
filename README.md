# Set a static IP for LAN connection:
#### 1. Go to Setting.
![WhatsApp Image 2023-07-12 at 10 16 54 AM - 複製](https://github.com/LHYWOOD/Terminal-Demo/assets/58466073/46fe8d47-4828-4484-83c5-808a15344792)

# Set a static IP for Wi-Fi connection:
#### 1. On the home page, go to the gear button.
![WhatsApp Image 2023-07-12 at 10 32 51 AM (1)](https://github.com/LHYWOOD/Terminal-Demo/assets/58466073/7e1eaf8f-4cbe-487a-8b4c-1ee244259665)
#### 2.
![WhatsApp Image 2023-07-12 at 10 40 15 AM](https://github.com/LHYWOOD/Terminal-Demo/assets/58466073/dc0e3948-49f4-4fb2-9d75-958a379b20f6)
#### 3. Select "Advanced options". In "IP settings", select "Static".
![WhatsApp Image 2023-07-12 at 10 19 39 AM](https://github.com/LHYWOOD/Terminal-Demo/assets/58466073/1294821e-0f53-49c4-9f7e-1e3eea6eebd7)
#### 4. Please insert your IP on the correct field. After that, click the "Connect" button.
![WhatsApp Image 2023-07-12 at 10 19 40 AM](https://github.com/LHYWOOD/Terminal-Demo/assets/58466073/4727b5f0-94e4-436b-923f-55d276af8216)
